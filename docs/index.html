About

Developed by UW Seattle Students, this is a space for you to informally reach out to get help on classes and/or to help other students succeed in their coursework. Use the form below to fill out the classes you have taken, as well as the classes you are currently taking. Filling this out will allow you to connect with other UW Seattle students who have taken the class that can help you with the classes you are currently taking.
